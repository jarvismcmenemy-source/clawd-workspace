# MEMORY.md - VALO Studios Context

## Business Model
- **Service:** AI implementation and transformation consulting
- **Target:** Businesses ready to adopt AI but need guidance
- **Pricing:** Project-based + retainer models
- **Differentiator:** Focus on implementation, not just strategy

## Key Offerings
1. AI Readiness Assessment → Lead gen + qualification
2. AI Strategy Workshops → High-ticket entry point
3. Implementation Projects → Core revenue
4. Ongoing Support → Recurring revenue

## Brand Voice
- Professional but approachable
- Future-focused, not fear-based
- Practical implementation over theory
- Results-oriented language

## Target Clients
- SMBs (10-200 employees) in service industries
- Marketing agencies needing AI integration
- Professional services firms (legal, accounting, consulting)
- Tech-enabled traditional businesses

## Key Metrics
- Assessment completions → Leads
- Lead → Discovery call conversion
- Discovery → Proposal conversion  
- Proposal → Close rate
- Project satisfaction scores

## Competitive Position
- vs Big consultancies: More agile, implementation-focused
- vs AI tools: Human guidance + customization
- vs freelancers: Professional process + scale

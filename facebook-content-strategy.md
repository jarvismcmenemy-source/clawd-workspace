### Primary Engine: Daily ChrisMc YouTube Videos
- **1 daily YouTube video** = 7 pieces of weekly anchor content
- Long-form, educational, business/AI focused
- High-value, searchable content for VALO lead generation

### Secondary Engine: Facebook Content Multiplication 
**Type A: YouTube Repurposing (4-5x per week)**
- Clip best 60-90 second segments from daily YouTube videos
- Native Facebook Reels with custom hooks
- Add Facebook-specific commentary/context

**Type B: Viral Content Commentary (2-3x per week)**
- Repurpose trending business/AI content with your insights
